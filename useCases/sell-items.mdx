---
title: Sell Items in Handcash Shop
description: 'Selling Items in the Handcash Shop'
---

To Promote your game in the Handcash Market, [create a payment request](/handcash-pay/create-payment-request) that represents a game item.  Once the payment request is paid, your application will receive a webhook and issueItems directly to the buyer.

Contact sales@handcash.io for assistance and to get your game items listed on the market.
